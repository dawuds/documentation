## 1. Purpose
Periodic reassessment of TPSPs per cadence in [STD-TP-01 Section 3.4](../03-standards/STD-TP-01-tpsp-due-diligence-standard.md) — annual for Tier 0/1; bi-annual for Tier 2; tri-annual for Tier 3.

## 2. Trigger
Calendar cadence per TPSP tier. Plus ad-hoc on material adverse change.

## 3. Procedure

| # | Actor | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | TPRM team | Identify TPSPs due for reassessment in upcoming quarter from [REG-TPS](../06-registers/REG-TPS-third-party-service-provider-register.md) |
| 2 | TPRM team | Issue reassessment questionnaire to TPSP per [STD-TP-03 Section 3.5](../03-standards/STD-TP-03-tpsp-continuous-monitoring-standard.md) |
| 3 | TPSP | Complete and return questionnaire with current attestation evidence |
| 4 | TPRM team | Review responses; cross-check with continuous monitoring outputs |
| 5 | CISO | Cyber assessment review per RMiT 10.49 for Tier 0/1 |
| 6 | Shariah Compliance (if Shariah-relevant) | Shariah review |
| 7 | TPRM team | Update risk rating; identify deteriorations |
| 8 | CRO | Approve reassessment outcome for Tier 0; CISO approves Tier 1; TPRM team approves Tier 2/3 |
| 9 | TPRM team | Update [REG-TPS](../06-registers/REG-TPS-third-party-service-provider-register.md); set next reassessment date |
| 10 | TPRM team | If material deterioration — engage TPSP for remediation; if egregious — initiate exit per [SOP-TP-03](SOP-TP-03-tpsp-exit-sop.md) |

## 4. Reporting
Quarterly TPRM team report to CRO + RMC: reassessments completed, deteriorations, exits initiated.

## 5. Evidence
Questionnaire responses; attestation evidence; reassessment outcome; CRO approval. Retained 7 years.
