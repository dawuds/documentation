## 1. Purpose
Operationalise TPSP onboarding — sourcing through go-live, with due diligence per [STD-TP-01](../03-standards/STD-TP-01-tpsp-due-diligence-standard.md).

## 2. Trigger
Business need for new TPSP engagement; approved sourcing case from Procurement.

## 3. Procedure

| # | Actor | Action | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Business requestor | Submit engagement request with use case, criticality assessment | Engagement request |
| 2 | Procurement | Source candidates; shortlist | Shortlist |
| 3 | Procurement + CRO | Determine TPSP criticality tier per [STD-TP-01 §3.2](../03-standards/STD-TP-01-tpsp-due-diligence-standard.md) | Tier assignment |
| 4 | Procurement + 2nd line (CISO, CRO, CCO, Shariah Compliance if relevant) | Execute due diligence per [STD-TP-01 §3.1](../03-standards/STD-TP-01-tpsp-due-diligence-standard.md) — RCM dimensions per Appendix 8 | DD evidence pack |
| 5 | CISO | Cyber assessment per RMiT 10.49 + [CRMF](../01-frameworks/CRMF.md) | Cyber risk score |
| 6 | Shariah Compliance (if relevant) | Shariah review per BNM SGF | Shariah review record |
| 7 | CRO | Approve material engagements; Procurement approves non-material | Approval |
| 8 | Procurement + Legal | Contract negotiation with required clauses per [POL-19](../02-policies/POL-19-supplier-security-policy.md) and STD-TP-02 (future) | Signed contract |
| 9 | CCO | If material outsourcing — BNM notification per BNM Outsourcing PD | BNM notification |
| 10 | Procurement | Create entry in [REG-TPS](../06-registers/REG-TPS-third-party-service-provider-register.md) | REG-TPS entry |
| 11 | Business + IT | Onboarding — access provisioning, integration, baseline monitoring | Operational handover |
| 12 | TPRM team | Continuous monitoring activated per [POL-10 §4.3](../02-policies/POL-10-it-vendor-management-policy.md) | Monitoring active |

## 4. Exception handling
Time-pressured onboarding: condensed DD with subsequent full DD within 90 days, approved by CRO. DD finding fail: engagement deferred until remediated or alternative TPSP sourced.

## 5. Evidence
DD evidence pack; contract; CRO approval; BNM notification (where material); REG-TPS entry. Retained 7 years post-engagement end per [STD-TP-01 §3.3](../03-standards/STD-TP-01-tpsp-due-diligence-standard.md).
