## Regulatory and statutory

| Acronym | Expansion | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **BNM** | Bank Negara Malaysia | Central bank and prudential regulator |
| **NACSA** | National Cyber Security Agency | Established under Cyber Security Act 2024; sector-lead for FS NCII |
| **PD** | Policy Document | BNM's formal regulatory issuances (e.g., BNM RMiT PD) |
| **RMiT** | Risk Management in Technology | BNM PD; current issuance 28 November 2025 |
| **CSA 2024** | Cyber Security Act 2024 | Malaysian law establishing NCII regime and NACSA |
| **NCII** | National Critical Information Infrastructure | Designation under CSA 2024 |
| **SGF** | Shariah Governance Framework | BNM PD for Islamic financial institutions |
| **MCIPD** | (BNM PD on) Management of Customer Information and Permitted Disclosures | Governs customer information handling |
| **BCM** | Business Continuity Management | BNM PD |
| **PDPA** | Personal Data Protection Act 2010 | Malaysian data protection law |
| **IFSA** | Islamic Financial Services Act 2013 | Licensing for Islamic FIs |
| **FSA** | Financial Services Act 2013 | Licensing for conventional FIs |
| **SC** | Securities Commission (Malaysia) | Capital markets regulator (referenced for cross-jurisdictional context) |
| **Cloud TRAG** | (BNM) Cloud Technology Risk Assessment Guidelines | Cloud-specific BNM guidance |
