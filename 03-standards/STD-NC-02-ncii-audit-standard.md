# STD-NC-02 — NCII Audit Standard

## 1. Purpose
Specify NCII-specific audit requirements per NACSA Code of Practice — internal audit coverage and external audit (where mandated).

## 2. Scope
Internal Audit cycles addressing NCII obligations. External NCII audit if mandated by NACSA.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 Internal NCII audit coverage
| Ref | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 3.1.1 | Internal Audit annual plan shall include NCII-scope coverage |
| 3.1.2 | Minimum coverage every NACSA Code of Practice major requirement on a 3-year cycle |
| 3.1.3 | All NCII-scope cyber control families audited at least every 2 years |

### 3.2 External NCII audit (where mandated)
| Ref | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 3.2.1 | Where NACSA mandates external audit (per Code), GIBB engages an audit firm approved by NACSA |
| 3.2.2 | External audit scope aligned to NACSA-specified expectations |
| 3.2.3 | Findings tracked through [REG-CAP](../06-registers/REG-CAP-corrective-action-register.md) and [REG-NCA NCII Audit Findings Register](../06-registers/REG-NCA-ncii-audit-findings-register.md) |

### 3.3 Audit reporting
| Ref | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 3.3.1 | Internal Audit reports to Board Audit Committee with NCII section flagged |
| 3.3.2 | Material findings reported to NACSA per Code of Practice expectations |
| 3.3.3 | All findings entered into [REG-NCA](../06-registers/REG-NCA-ncii-audit-findings-register.md) |

### 3.4 Remediation tracking
| Ref | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 3.4.1 | NCII audit findings remediated per agreed timelines |
| 3.4.2 | Overdue findings escalated to RMC + (where material) NACSA |

### 3.5 NACSA examination support
| Ref | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 3.5.1 | Internal Audit supports NACSA examination preparation |
| 3.5.2 | Per [SOP-NC-02 NACSA Examination Response SOP](../04-procedures/SOP-NC-02-nacsa-examination-response-sop.md) |

## 4. Exceptions
NCII audit obligations are not subject to exception within GIBB authority.
