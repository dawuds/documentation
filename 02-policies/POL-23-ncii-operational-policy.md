## 1. Purpose

To establish how GIBB discharges its obligations as a designated National Critical Information Infrastructure (NCII) entity under the **Cyber Security Act 2024** and the directives issued by **NACSA**. Operationalises [NCIIF](../01-frameworks/NCIIF.md) at the policy level. The underlying cyber controls are operated under [CRMF](../01-frameworks/CRMF.md); this policy adds NACSA-specific requirements.

## 2. Scope

All GIBB systems, processes, and personnel within the scope of NCII designation. All NACSA directives applicable to financial services NCII entities. NACSA notification channels, NCII audit requirements, NACSA examination cooperation.

## 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **NCII** | National Critical Information Infrastructure as designated under the Cyber Security Act 2024. |
| **CSA 2024** | Cyber Security Act 2024 (Malaysia). |
| **NACSA** | National Cyber Security Agency — sector lead for financial services NCII. |
| **NACSA Code of Practice** | The mandatory code applicable to NCII entities. |
| **NCII incident** | A cyber incident within NCII scope triggering NACSA notification distinct from BNM notification under RMiT 11.18. |

## 4. Policy statements

### 4.1 Code of Practice compliance

- **4.1.1** GIBB **shall** comply with all current NACSA Code of Practice provisions applicable to financial services NCII entities. *(Implements CSA 2024; RMiT 11.4.)*
- **4.1.2** New or amended NACSA directives **shall** be acknowledged and incorporated within the period specified by NACSA.

### 4.2 NCII designation maintenance

- **4.2.1** The scope of NCII designation **shall** be maintained accurately, with scope changes notified to NACSA per Code of Practice requirements.

### 4.3 NCII notification

- **4.3.1** NCII incidents **shall** be notified to NACSA per current NACSA directives, **in addition to** BNM notification under RMiT 11.18. *(Implements CSA 2024; RMiT 11.4.)*
- **4.3.2** The CCO maintains the current NACSA notification timelines, channels, and form, and ensures the on-call team has access.

### 4.4 NCII audit

- **4.4.1** GIBB **shall** undergo NCII audit per NACSA requirements — internal audit aligned to NACSA-specified scope and frequency; external audit where mandated.

### 4.5 NACSA examination cooperation

- **4.5.1** GIBB **shall** cooperate fully with NACSA examinations — provide evidence, access to systems, remediation of findings within agreed timelines.

### 4.6 Control elevation

- **4.6.1** Where the NACSA Code of Practice specifies controls beyond [CRMF](../01-frameworks/CRMF.md) baseline, the elevated control **shall** apply within NCII scope.

## 6. Exceptions

NCII Code of Practice obligations are **not subject to exception** within GIBB authority. Where compliance is materially constrained, escalate to NACSA per Code procedure.

## 7. Enforcement

Per TRMF Section 12. Non-compliance with NACSA directives is material disciplinary matter.
