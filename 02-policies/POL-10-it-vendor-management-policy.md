## 1. Purpose

To establish how GIBB selects, contracts with, onboards, monitors, and exits IT vendors and third-party service providers (TPSPs). Operationalises [TPRMF](../01-frameworks/TPRMF.md) at the IT-vendor scope, complementing [POL-19 Supplier and Third-Party Security Policy](POL-19-supplier-security-policy.md) which addresses information security obligations.

## 2. Scope

All IT vendors and TPSPs providing services or products to GIBB — IaaS, PaaS, SaaS, managed services, software vendors, IT consultancies, IT contractors. Cloud-specific risk content is in [CloudRMF](../01-frameworks/CloudRMF.md); this policy governs the relationship lifecycle.

## 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **TPSP** | Third Party Service Provider per RMiT 10.46–10.49. |
| **Material outsourcing** | Per BNM Outsourcing PD — outsourcing whose failure would significantly disrupt GIBB or affect compliance. |
| **Risk Coverage Matrix (RCM)** | Risk categories considered in TPSP due diligence per RMiT Appendix 8. |
| **Sub-contracting** | TPSP engagement of further parties (4th parties) to fulfil obligations. |

## 4. Policy statements

### 4.1 Risk-based engagement

- **4.1.1** Every IT vendor engagement **shall** be risk-assessed before onboarding per RMiT 10.47, applying the Appendix 8 Risk Coverage Matrix.
- **4.1.2** Material outsourcing **shall** comply with BNM Outsourcing PD requirements, including notification or approval obligations.

### 4.2 Contractual obligations

- **4.2.1** IT vendor contracts **shall** incorporate security obligations per [POL-19](POL-19-supplier-security-policy.md), service-level commitments, audit rights, sub-contracting controls, data return / destruction on exit, and applicable regulatory obligations. *(Implements RMiT 10.48.)*
- **4.2.2** Right-to-audit clauses **shall** be included in material vendor contracts, exercisable by GIBB or its agent and (where applicable) BNM. *(Implements RMiT 10.46.)*

### 4.3 Continuous monitoring

- **4.3.1** TPSP performance and security posture **shall** be monitored continuously per RMiT 10.49 — incident notification from TPSP, attestation/certification monitoring (ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type 2), periodic reassessment by criticality.

### 4.4 Concentration

- **4.4.1** TPSP concentration risk **shall** be monitored and managed within risk appetite per [POL-03](POL-03-technology-risk-appetite-statement.md).

### 4.5 Exit

- **4.5.1** Every material TPSP contract **shall** include an exit strategy covering data return / destruction, knowledge transfer, and orderly transition.

### 4.6 Shariah considerations

- **4.6.1** Outsourcing arrangements touching Islamic finance product systems **shall** undergo Shariah review per BNM Shariah Governance Framework. Shariah Committee approval is required for material outsourcing of Shariah-relevant functions.
