## 1. Purpose

To define personnel security controls applied before, during, and after employment or engagement, ensuring that personnel are screened, trained, and contractually bound to protect GIBB information.

## 2. Scope

All employees, contractors, secondees, interns, and third-party personnel with access to GIBB information assets.

## 3. Policy statements

### 3.1 Pre-employment screening

- **3.1.1** Personnel shall be subject to screening proportionate to the sensitivity of their role and to legal requirements. Privileged or sensitive roles shall require enhanced screening (background, financial probity, references). *(Implements ISO/IEC 27002:2022 control 6.1.)*

### 3.2 Terms of employment

- **3.2.1** Information security responsibilities shall be set out in the contract of employment or service. *(Implements ISO/IEC 27002:2022 control 6.2.)*
- **3.2.2** All personnel shall sign a confidentiality or non-disclosure agreement before access to Confidential or Highly Restricted information is granted. *(Implements ISO/IEC 27002:2022 control 6.6.)*

### 3.3 Awareness and training

- **3.3.1** All personnel shall complete information security awareness training within 30 days of joining and annually thereafter. *(Implements ISO/IEC 27002:2022 control 6.3; BNM RMiT Section 15 (Security Awareness and Education).)*
- **3.3.2** High-risk roles (developers, system administrators, SOC analysts, customer-facing staff handling sensitive data) shall receive role-specific training in addition to general awareness.
- **3.3.3** Awareness shall be continuous — phishing simulations, communications, and event-based reinforcement — not annual-only.

### 3.4 Disciplinary process

- **3.4.1** A formal disciplinary process shall apply to information security violations, proportionate to the nature and impact of the violation. *(Implements ISO/IEC 27002:2022 control 6.4.)*

### 3.5 Termination and change of role

- **3.5.1** On termination or change of role, access shall be modified or revoked per [POL-06 Access Control Policy](POL-06-access-control-policy.md), assets returned per [POL-09 IT Asset Management Policy](POL-09-it-asset-management-policy.md), and continuing confidentiality obligations communicated. *(Implements ISO/IEC 27002:2022 control 6.5.)*
