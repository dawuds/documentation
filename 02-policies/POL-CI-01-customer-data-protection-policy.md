## 1. Purpose

To establish how GIBB collects, uses, discloses, retains, and destroys customer data in compliance with MCIPD, PDPA 2010, and the bank's Shariah obligations. Operationalises [CIMF](../01-frameworks/CIMF.md) at the policy level.

## 2. Scope

All customer information held by GIBB — personal data (PDPA scope), customer financial data, transaction data, authentication data, correspondence, instructions. All channels, all forms.

## 3. Definitions

See [CIMF Section 4](../01-frameworks/CIMF.md). Additional terms:

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Data subject** | The customer to whom personal data relates. |
| **Records of Processing Activity (ROPA)** | The PDPA-aligned register of customer-data processing operations. |

## 4. Policy statements

### 4.1 Lawful basis

- **4.1.1** Every processing of customer personal data **shall** have an identified lawful basis under PDPA — typically customer consent, contractual necessity, or legitimate interest. The basis **shall** be recorded in the [Records of Processing Activity](../06-registers/REG-ROPA-records-of-processing-activity.md). *(Implements PDPA Section 6.)*

### 4.2 Confidentiality default

- **4.2.1** Customer information **shall** be treated as Confidential or Highly Restricted by default per [DGF](../01-frameworks/DGF.md) classification, and disclosed only as permitted by MCIPD or other authorising legal basis. *(Implements BNM MCIPD.)*

### 4.3 Permitted disclosures

- **4.3.1** Disclosures of customer information **shall** be limited to those permitted by MCIPD. Disclosures **shall** be logged in the Disclosure Register.

### 4.4 Data subject rights

- **4.4.1** GIBB **shall** respond to customer data subject requests (access, correction, consent withdrawal) within statutory timeframes under PDPA. *(Implements PDPA Sections 30–37.)*

### 4.5 Breach notification

- **4.5.1** Customer data breaches with material impact **shall** be assessed against the notification requirements of PDPA and, where relevant, BNM expectations per Operational Risk Reporting PD. *(Implements PDPA + BNM ORR PD.)*

### 4.6 Retention

- **4.6.1** Customer data **shall** be retained only as long as required by business need, contractual obligation, regulatory record-keeping requirements, or legal hold. Retention schedules documented per data category. *(Implements PDPA Section 10.)*

### 4.7 Cross-border

- **4.7.1** Transfer of customer data outside Malaysia **shall** comply with PDPA cross-border transfer requirements. *(Implements PDPA Section 129.)*

### 4.8 Customer authentication

- **4.8.1** Customer authentication to digital channels **shall** comply with BNM RMiT Section 12 and Appendices 2 and 3 minimum security controls.

### 4.9 Shariah-confidential customer data

- **4.9.1** Customer data classified Shariah-confidential **shall** be handled with additional restrictions per the Shariah Governance Framework.
