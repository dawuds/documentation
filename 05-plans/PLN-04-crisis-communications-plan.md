## 1. Purpose
To coordinate internal, customer, regulator, media, and public communication during crises — cyber incidents, service disruptions, data breaches, reputational events — preserving customer trust, regulatory standing, and bank reputation.

## 2. Scope

All crisis events requiring coordinated external communication, including SEV-1 cyber incidents, material BCP/DRP activations, material customer data breaches (per [SOP-CI-02](../04-procedures/SOP-CI-02-customer-data-breach-notification-sop.md)), regulator-driven public events, NACSA-driven public events.

## 3. Activation

| Trigger | Authority |
|---|---|
| SEV-1 cyber incident with customer or regulatory implications | CISO + CEO joint decision |
| Material BCP activation affecting customer-facing services | COO + CEO |
| Customer data breach requiring customer notification | DPO + CCO + CEO |
| Media enquiry or social-media event requiring response | Head of Corp Comms + CEO |
| Regulator-driven public statement requirement | CEO + General Counsel |

## 4. Roles

| Role | Default | Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| Crisis Communications Lead | Head of Corp Comms | Owns external comms strategy and execution |
| CEO | CEO | Final approval of public statements; spokesperson for material events |
| CISO | CISO | Technical accuracy for cyber-related comms |
| General Counsel | GC | Legal review of all external comms |
| CCO | CCO | Regulatory implications of comms |
| DPO | DPO | Customer data implications |
| Customer Operations Lead | Head of Customer Ops | Customer enquiry handling |
| Social Media Lead | Designated marketing officer | Social channels monitoring + response |
| Investor Relations | Head of IR | Listed-entity disclosure obligations if applicable |
| Shariah Liaison | Head of Shariah Compliance | Shariah implications messaging if relevant |

## 5. Communication audiences and approval matrix

| Audience | Approval | Trigger | Channel |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Internal — all staff** | CEO + Head of Corp Comms | Crisis affecting staff or visible | Intranet, internal email, all-staff calls |
| **Customers — affected only** | DPO + GC + Head of Corp Comms | Per [SOP-CI-02](../04-procedures/SOP-CI-02-customer-data-breach-notification-sop.md) or service-disruption SLAs | Email, SMS, in-app secure message, letter |
| **Customers — all** | CEO + GC | Major service disruption or breach | Website, mobile app, branches |
| **Bank Negara Malaysia** | CCO + CRO + (CISO for cyber) | Per [POL-13 §4.4.3](../02-policies/POL-13-incident-management-policy.md) RMiT 11.18 (4-hour clock with source-chain caveat) | Per BNM-prescribed channel |
| **NACSA** | CCO + CISO | Per [STD-NC-01](../03-standards/STD-NC-01-nacsa-notification-standard.md) | Per NACSA channel |
| **PDPA Commissioner** | DPO + CCO | Per PDPA assessment | Per Commissioner channel |
| **Other regulators** (SC, Inland Revenue, etc.) | CCO + GC | Per applicability | Per regulator channel |
| **Media — press release** | CEO + GC | Major public event | Wire service + own channels |
| **Media — enquiry response** | Head of Corp Comms | Reactive | Direct response |
| **Social channels** | Social Media Lead with Corp Comms direction | Real-time monitoring | Official accounts |
| **Investors** (if listed) | CEO + IR + GC | Per disclosure obligations | Bursa / equivalent |
| **Shariah Committee** | Head of Shariah Compliance | If affecting Shariah-compliant services materially | Direct |

## 6. Communication discipline

| Rule | Rationale |
|---|---|
| Single spokesperson per event (CEO for material) | Consistency |
| No speculation; only confirmed facts | Credibility |
| Customer notifications drafted in plain language | Customer comprehension |
| All public statements legal-reviewed | Liability protection |
| No team member outside authorised list speaks to press | Message control |
| Holding statements pre-prepared (per scenario class) | Speed |

## 7. Pre-prepared templates

(Maintained as Appendix A; templates per scenario class):

- Holding statement — service disruption (Tier 0 / Tier 1 / Tier 2)
- Customer notification — data breach (sensitive personal data / financial data / authentication data)
- Internal staff notification — cyber incident with do-not-click ask
- BNM notification template (in coordination with [SOP-NC-01](../04-procedures/SOP-NC-01-nacsa-incident-notification-sop.md))
- Press statement — confirmed material event
- Social media response — speculative incident posts
